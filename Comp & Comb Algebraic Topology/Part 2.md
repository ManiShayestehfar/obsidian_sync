## Vietoris-Rips

Let $(M,d)$ be a finite metric space. $VR_\varepsilon(M)$ is defined as follows:

A set $\{x_0,x_2,...,x_k\}\subset M$ forms a $k$-simplex iff $d(x_i,x_j)\leq \varepsilon$ for all $i,j$.
