>$$\cdots \overset{}{\longrightarrow}C_n' \overset{(i_n,f_n')}{\longrightarrow}C_n \oplus D_n' \overset{(f_n-j_n)}{\longrightarrow} D_b \overset{\Delta_n}{\longrightarrow}C_{n-1}' \overset{(i_{n-1},f_{n-1}')}{\longrightarrow}\cdots$$
