# Definition 

>[!Definition] Quotient Space
Let $(X,\|\cdot\|_p)$ be a metric space and $Y \subset X$
> $$X/Y := \{x + Y \:|\: x \in X\}$$
> such that for $\lambda \in \mathbb{K}$ and $x,x' \in X$
> 1. $\lambda(x+ Y) = (\lambda x) + Y$
> 2. $(x+Y) + (x'+Y) = (x+x') + Y$

## Normed Space of a Quotient Space

>[!Theorem]
>If $Y$ is closed, then $X/Y$ is a normed vector space with $\|x+Y\| = \inf_{y \in Y}\|x-y\|_p$.
>Moreover, if $X$ is Banach $\implies Y$ is Banach.
