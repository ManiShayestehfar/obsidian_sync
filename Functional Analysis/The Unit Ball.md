Closed ball: $\bar{B}(0,1) = \{x \in X \:|\: \|x\| < 1\}$.

>[!Theorem]
>$\dim X < \infty \iff \bar{B}(0,1)$ is compact.
##### Proof
###### $(\implies)$ 
Recall that a finite dimensional normed space is Banach, and therefore it is complete. Since $\bar{B}$ is closed in $X$, $\bar{B}$ is complete (as a closed subset of a complete metric space).
